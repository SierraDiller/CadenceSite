ABOUT

Cadence Gates is a passionate and versatile musical theater actress with extensive training and a dynamic stage presence. She has performed in acclaimed productions such as "RENT," "Chicago," and "Wicked," earning praise for her vocal range and emotional depth. Cadence holds a BFA in Musical Theater and has trained in classical and contemporary dance. Her special skills include a powerful mezzo-soprano range, advanced jazz and tap dance, and dialect work.
